Research strategy

Our Research Strategy outlines how PHE uses research, translation and innovation to support the transformation of public health practice and services.

This strategy outlines PHE plans, actions and measures of success for research on public and population health and health protection. It outlines our commitment to working in partnership with academics, research funders, the NHS and the public.

We foster, encourage and enable research to address priority questions for public health, align research capacity to priorities and support the translation of findings into innovations in practices and services.

Read our research strategy

Strategic priorities

  1. Generate new knowledge to improve and protect health
  2. Build, share and sustain high-quality infrastructure to enable research by PHE and others
  3. Support development of public health research capacity in PHE and elsewhere and inspire public health research careers
  4. Accelerate translation of research into policy and practice in public health and commercial products
  5. Communicate widely and openly about how research is contributing to improving and protecting health and wellbeing and reducing health inequalities
